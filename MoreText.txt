Harry Potter is a beloved character from a series of fantasy novels written by British author JK Rowling.
The series follows the life of Harry Potter, a young wizard, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley as they attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
The books are set in a magical world where witches and wizards exist and are often faced with dangers from the dark wizard, Lord Voldemort.
The series consists of seven books, each chronicling a year in Harry's life at Hogwarts.
The books have been adapted into a successful film franchise, with eight movies in total.
Harry Potter has become a cultural phenomenon, with millions of fans worldwide and a dedicated fanbase known as Potterheads.
